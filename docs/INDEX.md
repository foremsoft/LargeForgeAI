## Document Conventions

### Status Indicators
- **Draft**: Under development, may change significantly
- **Review**: Ready for review, feedback welcome
- **Approved**: Officially approved and stable
- **Deprecated**: Being phased out

### Version Information
All documents include:
- Last updated date
- Version (for specifications)
- Author/maintainer where applicable

### Feedback
Found an issue or want to suggest an improvement?
- Open an issue on [GitHub](https://github.com/largeforgeai/largeforgeai/issues)
- Tag with `documentation` label
